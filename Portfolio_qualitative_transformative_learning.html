Qualitative Research on Transformative Learning in Engineering Design Innovation

TAG: Field Study, Interview, Observation, Qualitative data analysis, Design Innovation, Engineering Education

Link to the full paper

WHAT:
Qualitative study during PhD
WHY:
How do engineering learners develop design innovation knowledge and mentalities?
HOW:
The natural transitioning from an engineering science-based mentality to a new design mindset presents a unique opportunity to study engineers' growth experiences and identity reestablishment, which has been fairly obscure in literature.
